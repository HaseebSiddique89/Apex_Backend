Q1. How many chambers does the human heart have?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
Answer: c

Q2. What are the upper chambers of the heart called?
a) Aorta
b) Atrium
c) Vena cava
d) Ventricle
Answer: b

Q3. Which chamber pumps oxygen-rich blood to the body?
a) Left atrium
b) Right atrium
c) Left ventricle
d) Right ventricle
Answer: c

Q4. What is the function of the tricuspid valve?
a) Controls blood flow from the left atrium to the left ventricle
b) Controls blood flow from the right ventricle to the pulmonary artery
c) Controls blood flow from the right atrium to the right ventricle
d) None of the above
Answer: c

Q5. Which blood vessel carries oxygenated blood from the lungs to the left atrium?
a) Pulmonary artery
b) Pulmonary veins
c) Aorta
d) Superior vena cava
Answer: b
