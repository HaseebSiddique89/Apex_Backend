Q1. What is the name of the heart's natural pacemaker?
a) Sinoatrial (SA) node
b) Atrioventricular node
c) Pericardium
d) Aorta
Answer: a)

Q2. How many chambers does the heart have?
a) 2
b) 4
c) 6
d) 8
Answer: b)

Q3. Which valve separates the left ventricle from the aorta?
a) Tricuspid valve
b) Mitral valve
c) Pulmonary valve
d) Aortic valve
Answer: d)

Q4. What is the function of the coronary arteries?
a) To supply deoxygenated blood to the lungs
b) To carry oxygenated blood away from the heart
c) To supply the heart muscle with oxygenated blood
d) To regulate blood pressure in the body
Answer: c)

Q5. Which statement best describes the role of the heart in the human body?
a) The heart is responsible for filtering and purifying the blood.
b) The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body.
c) The heart produces red blood cells and platelets.
d) The heart regulates hormone production in the endocrine system.
Answer: b)
