Q1. How many chambers does the human heart have?
a) 2
b) 4
c) 6
d) 8

Answer: b)

Q2. What is the function of the right ventricle?
a) Pumps oxygenated blood to the body
b) Receives deoxygenated blood from the lungs
c) Receives oxygenated blood from the lungs
d) Pumps deoxygenated blood to the lungs

Answer: d)

Q3. What are the layers of the heart wall called, from outer to inner layer?
a) Endocardium, Myocardium, Epicardium
b) Epicardium, Myocardium, Endocardium
c) Myocardium, Endocardium, Epicardium
d) Endocardium, Epicardium, Myocardium

Answer: b)
